This revision includes significant changes to the Logistics Readiness (LR) Quality Assurance Program and must be reviewed in its
entirety.
All supplements and addendums are submitted to Air Force Directorate of Logistics, Engineering, and Force Protection (AF/A4L) for
approval.
1.1.1.Repair Network Integration is an Enterprise Logistics Governance chartered initiative designed as an incremental approach to
integrate maintenance and materiel management functions and establish an enterprise approach to repair.
Major changes include adding Major Command (MAJCOM) senior enlisted logistics planner’s roles and responsibilities and reiterating
Air Force Installation and Mission Support Center (AFIMSC) roles and responsibilities.
